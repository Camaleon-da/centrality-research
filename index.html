What we do

We consult with your team to create customized solutions that are right for your organization and community; co-developing a roadmap with you using participatory action research, appreciative inquiry, human centered design and other culturally responsive research methodologies to set you up for success in both your short- and long-term goals. Community connects everything we do. Let us help you forge that connection with your community to build a stronger organization and a more inclusive future.

We believe in the power of outcomes and goals. Our team can help you identify the outcomes of your program and develop tools to measure against them. We employ interactive data collection techniques, culturally-responsive evaluation methods, and provide clear and impactful reports.

  • We have developed a unique Co-Creation Process that will support your desires to bring more and diverse voices into the decision-making process to ensure relevancy and connection with your organization and your products.
  • We will work with you to map a community engagement process for your project and establish benchmarks with quantitative and qualitative measures. This allows for opportunities to reflect, adjust strategies, and understand how your efforts are paying off.

Staff and Stakeholders are important communities. Our team can support your efforts to gain a deeper understanding of internal and external perceptions of your business culture. We will work with you to determine areas for focus and growth and provide you with ongoing benchmarking to determine the impacts of your efforts.

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion should be in everything we do, but we know it can be hard to understand what the most impactful first steps are, how to measure change effectively, and how to create a DEI plan that is more than lip service but is actionable change embraced at every level. Let us work with you to increase participation in your DEI efforts, collaboratively determine your DEI goals, articulate the best strategies to begin (or continue) to move forward, and create measures to ensure progress and accountability.
